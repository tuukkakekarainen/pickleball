🥒 Official Pickleball Rules & Court
Court Dimensions
  • Court: 20 ft × 44 ft
  • Non-Volley Zone (Kitchen): 7 ft from net on each side
  • Net height: 36 in at sidelines, 34 in at centre
Scoring
  • Games are played to 11 points, win by 2.
  • Only the serving side can score a point (traditional) — or rally scoring (each rally scores).
  • In doubles, each team gets two serve turns per rotation (except the first serve of the game).
  • Score is announced as: Server score – Receiver score – Server number (e.g. 5-3-2).
Serving Rules
  • Serve must be made underhand with paddle contact below waist level.
  • Server must have at least one foot behind the baseline.
  • Serve must land in the diagonal service court (beyond the kitchen).
  • There is one serve attempt (no second serve) unless a let occurs.
The Double-Bounce Rule
  • After the serve the ball must bounce once on each side before volleys are allowed.
  • The serve receiver must let the ball bounce, then the serving team must also let it bounce before volleying.
Non-Volley Zone (Kitchen)
  • Players cannot volley (hit the ball in the air) while standing in the kitchen.
  • You may enter the kitchen to play a ball that has bounced there.
  • Momentum carrying you into the kitchen after a volley is a fault.
Faults
  • Ball lands out of bounds.
  • Ball hits the net.
  • Volleying from the kitchen.
  • Ball bounces twice before being returned.
  • Serving into the wrong court or into the kitchen.
